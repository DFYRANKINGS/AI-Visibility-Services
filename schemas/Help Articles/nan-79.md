### Guide
### Why License Reporting Matters
License reporting allows assistants to verify your credentials automatically through transparent structured data.

### How to Report
- Include License or Credential schema.
- Add issuing agency, URL, and expiration date.
- Update reports quarterly.

### Benefit
Consistent license reporting enhances trust and keeps your brand eligible for AI-based professional listings.
