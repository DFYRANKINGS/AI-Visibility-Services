### Guide
### Why a Validation Guide Helps
Local schema validation guide gives a clear process for testing regional schema files before AI indexing.

### Validation Steps
- Use schema.org validator for each city page.
- Confirm geo coordinates and address structure.
- Check timestamp freshness.

### Benefit
A detailed local schema validation guide prevents errors and improves local AI recognition.
